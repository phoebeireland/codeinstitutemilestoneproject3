About Us

Rate Venture is a community built to inspire and connect outdoor enthusiasts looking for their next adventure. We recognise that when travelling to a new location, it is sometimes difficult to find the perfect outdoor company that fits you or your family's needs. We are here to help you find a reputable company that will be the perfect fit for your next outdoor excurstion, whether you're looking for guided hikes, rock climbing, white water rafting or more!

*At the moment, Rate Venture is operating mainly from the US, but we are working on expanding our area to the EU.*
